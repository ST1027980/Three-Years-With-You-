Three Years Together

Three years ago, I chose you.

I didn’t know then that you would become my calm in chaos,
my comfort in silence,
my safest place in this world.

I don’t just love you.
I love that you love me.

The beginning — when everything was new, but you already felt right.
The growth — when I realized you weren’t just part of my life… you were my peace.
Now — and I still look at you the same way.

No matter what life brings,
I am grateful it brought me you.
